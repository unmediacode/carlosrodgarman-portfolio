GOSPEL SYMPHONY

Gospel Symphony, the fourth album by «Black Light Gospel Choir».

Gospel Symphony is the fourth album by the Black Light Gospel Choir. Gospel Symphony, fuses gospel and symphonic music. It was recorded at Eastwest Studios in Los Angeles and mixed at RG Music. A hundred people from different continents have collaborated in the work, all coordinated by Rebeca Rods and Carlos Rodgarman. Some of the tracks were mixed in Dolby Atmos and for his second volume, which he hopes to release next year, all the work will be in 7.1.4.

MIXED AT RG MUSIC BY CARLOS RODGARMAN AND PRODUCED BY REBECA RODS & CARLOS RODGARMAN
