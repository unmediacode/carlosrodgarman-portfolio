Biography

Born in Vigo Spain, Carlos Rodgarman is a GRAMMY nominated Engineer as well as a Musical Producer and Arranger who has worked on several award-winning albums including Luis Fonsi's "Vida" (2019), Josh Groban's "Stages", and Demi Lovato's "Éxhale".

Rodgarman has toured the world as a Musical Director, Arranger and Pianist working with renowned artists such as Celine Dion, All-4-One, Sheila E., and Arturo Sandoval.

Carlos is the Founder of RG Music and RG Studios, a top recording studio in Los Angeles producing high-end recordings and Dolby Atmos mixes using a rare 48 input Neve Genesys Black console.

His debut album "The Rodgarband" assembled an impressive collective of International Musicians and Composers reflecting his ability to bring together diverse musical styles.
