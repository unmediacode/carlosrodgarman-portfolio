The Essential

MICHAEL BUBLÉ

Michael Bublé's first immersive collection.

The Essential Michael Bublé, was released on 12 August this year. RG Music was the studio in charge of mixing the talented artist's new work.

RG Music recently installed the 7.1.4 Atmos monitoring system at his facility, comprising an array of Genelec Smart Active Monitors. Genelec 8341A LCR speakers, four 8331A speakers as the left and right side and rear surrounds, and four 8320A speakers overhead. A 7370A subwoofer is matched to the LCR array and a 7360A sub is matched to the surround and overhead speakers.

MIXED AT RG MUSIC BY CARLOS RODGARMAN AND PRODUCER HUMBERTO GATICA
